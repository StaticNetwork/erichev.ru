  • Modern stressful situations and feelings of anxiety

    This information is aimed to people who experience anxiety and want to learn how to resist it. It may be particularly relevant now, when the world is changing rapidly, and TV news are like a military hostilities. There are the financial crisis, the threat of layoff, rising costs, and uncertainty about the future. Anxiety rises inside of us, and number of people have thought like a «and it will be even worse.» And this uncertain threat of the future begins to spoil the current life. How to survive this time? How to get over this stage with minimal losses for themselves and loved ones?

  • Anxiety and phobic disorders

    Anxiety is an emotional feeling of the discomfort based on uncertainty of future. Evolutionary, anxiety helps us mobilize resources to ensure appropriate behavior in extreme situations. Adaptive role of anxiety has been described by U. Cannon, who called it "fight-or-flight response". Caveman could either attack or run meeting with danger. When a human was in danger, he had a fear with adrenaline released into the blood, the heart began to beat faster, blood flow in the muscles and the heart was increased, blood clotting was increased to ensure minimum loss of blood in case of injury, and blood glucose level rose to provide for body optimal conditions to increase activity.

  • Tobacco dependence

If smoking is a bad habit, then it can be easily refused with only a small effort. Unfortunately, it's not like that. Smoker needs to make considerable efforts to change and maintain a new lifestyle without tobacco.

  • Gambling

    Gambling (scientifically, is a pathologic gambling) is currently a common disease in our country. It is a disease, not an infirmity of character, whim or indulgence. Pathologic gambling is a chronic relapsing mental disorder.

  • Alcohol addiction

    Why do many patients alcohol become such a serious problem? Some people try using alcohol to adjust their state of mind (to improve mood, reduce anxiety, to restore the sleep cycle, to escape from everyday problems). And it seems that alcohol helps, and the problem of alcoholism seems illusory and relating to anyone but themselves.
