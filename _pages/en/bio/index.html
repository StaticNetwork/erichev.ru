Autobiography (curriculum vitae)

Diplomas

Erichev Aleksandr Nikolaevich is a psychotherapist, candidate of medical sciences, leading researcher at the Department of Biopsychosocial Rehabilitation in the Mental Disorders of Bekhterev Psychoneurological Research Institute, Associate Professor of Chair of psychotherapy and sexology of North-Western State Medical University named after I.I. Mechnikov, co-director of the section Cognitive Behavioral Therapy in Russian Association of Psychotherapy, a member of the coordinating council of the Russian Association of Psychotherapy. He is a certified Schema therapist.

In 2000 he graduated with honors from the Military Medical Academy, followed by postgraduate psychiatric education in Bekhterev Psychoneurological Research Institute — internship training of Psychiatry from 2000 until 2001, and then full-time postgraduate study from 2001 until 2004.

Aleksandr Nikolaevich underwent training courses on psychotherapy, sexology, psychiatry and narcology at the leading scientific institutions of Russia. At the same time he executed and defended his thesis for the degree of Candidate of Medical Sciences (Head — prof. Aleksandr Petrovich Kotsyubinsky). He worked as a junior researcher in the department of community psychiatry at Bekhterev Psychoneurological Research Institute. Since February 2006 he worked as an assistant of the Department of Psychotherapy at Saint-Petersburg Medical Academy of Postgraduate Studies; and since 2009 — Associate Professor of this Chair. Since March 2011 to the present time he works as a leading researcher of Bekhterev Psychoneurological Research Institute.

Alexander is actively engaged in research activities. He has become the recipient of several national and citywide scientific competitions (2002 — 3rd prize in the competition for young scientists performed as a part of Bekhterev’s readings; 2003 — 2nd place in the competition, performed as a part of first all-Russian school of young scientists and psychiatrists; 2004 — 2nd prize in the competition for young scientists performed as a part Bekhterev’s readings; 2006 — 2nd place in the competition of scientific and practical studies of young scientists and specialists in mental health of the Russian Society of Psychiatrists.

In 2006 he underwent the training of lecturers in Hungary, performed by marked experts in mental health (prof. Montgomery, prof. Casper).

In 2008 he completed the training at the University Hospital «Eppendorf» in Hamburg, where he adopted experience of colleagues in treatment of patients with anxiety and depressive disorders and addictions.

In 2010 he completed certified training «The treatment of nicotine addiction» at the Mayo Clinic (USA, Rochester), a center for the treatment of nicotine addiction and a leading scientific institution according this problem in the world.

In May 2011 he gave a report in first International Congress of German-Russian Society of Psychiatry, Psychotherapy and Psychosocial health in Nurnberg.

In autumn 2011 first separate symposium of cognitive-behavioral therapy under the supervision of Erichev A. was performed, and in the fall of 2012 the first all-Russian conference with international participation «Cognitive-behavioral therapy: yesterday, today, and tomorrow» was conducted.

In October 2014 he was invited to give a presentation at the international symposium (Culture-sensitive patient information material, International symposium) at the University of Freiburg. During this trip he had an opportunity of direct acquaintance with the work of colleagues in a number of leading clinics of Germany.

In November 2014 he made a presentation «Development of cognitive-behavioral psychotherapy in Russia» at the Congress DGPPN (German Association for Psychiatry, Psychotherapy and Psychosomatics) in Berlin.

In 2015, he made a report at the 15th International Congress of the European Association of Cognitive-behavioral Psychotherapy (45th Annual EABCT Congress. Jerusalem, Israel, August 31 — September 3, 2015).

Erichev A.N. has published more than 70 articles related to the use of psychotherapeutic interventions. He also is the author of several monographs pointed to people with symptoms of anxiety, depression and addictions.

Aleksandr Nikolaevich, in cooperation with Dudko Taras Nikolaevich and Belokrylov Igor Vladlenovich, developed a comprehensive program for the treatment of patients with alcohol dependence named «Point of Sobriety».

In 2011 with the assistance of Aleksandr Henrikhovich Sofronov he developed and published practical manuals for training doctors in basic psychotherapeutic skills to work with smoking patients; he also has written a workbook to facilitate smokers go through the stages of nicotine withdrawal. He conducts training sessions among medical doctors aimed at training skills to treat addicted people.

In cooperation with Aleksandr Petrovich Fedorov he performs adaptation to Russian environment of cognitive-behavioral psychotherapy programs for overweight people.

A number of dissertation researches on cognitive-behavioral psychotherapy for different conditions ruled by Aleksandr Nikolaevich.

Field of interests — psychotherapy, individual and group psychotherapy, organizational consulting. The predominant activity is CBT in addictions, anxiety and depressive disorder, and endogenous mental disorders.

Aleksandr Nikolaevich considers increasing quality of life of people seeking psychological help as a main purpose of his practice.
